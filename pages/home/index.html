How I got into UX

The first time I came to realise the impact of design on the tools used by an organisation was when I got hired to support medical staffs during the deployment of a new patient management system across the whole university hospital of Montpellier in France back in summer 2012. We were all assigned on site (making it easier for staffs to reach out to us) and most of the issues we had to help with were related to confusing design choices that made the user journey complicated rather than bugs.
