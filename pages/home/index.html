Design management / Design ops

I can support you by:

  • Optimising your design process so your teams can work efficiently
  • Managing risk and ensuring proper delivery that complies with requirements
  • Implementing change within product teams and building communities of practices

Looking for some help or input?

Having a multi-disciplinary team working on a product or a project can come with some trade-offs. It might be due to a design process issue, a lack of decision making at certain checkpoints or the fact your people skillset is not being used adequately. This is where I can help you getting it right.
